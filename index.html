About Unmanned Systems Engineering

We are an engineering-first organization dedicated to advancing unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) technology. Our focus lies in the complete design lifecycle, from conceptualization and composite structures to autonomous systems integration and flight testing.

Our mission is twofold: to develop sovereign UAV capabilities that solve real-world challenges and to nurture the next generation of engineering talent through hands-on, community-driven projects.

Key Focus Areas

  • Long-range logistics UAVs
  • 24/7 surveillance VTOL systems
  • Hobby FPV & education kits
  • Research & industry collaboration

Core Platforms

From heavy-lift logistics to 24/7 surveillance and hobby FPV.

B

BADANG – Logistics UAV

A heavy-lift VTOL platform designed for long-range logistics, ideal for island, offshore, and remote area deliveries.

  • Range: 100km+
  • Payload: 10 kg
  • Propulsion: Hybrid VTOL
  • Use Cases: Medical supplies, critical parts
Learn More
M

MAHSURI – Surveillance VTOL

An autonomous, 24/7 surveillance solution with an automated battery-swapping base station for persistent monitoring.

  • Endurance: 4+ hours per flight
  • Automation: Fully autonomous missions
  • Missions: Border security, industrial inspection
Learn More
H

Hobby FPV & Education

Accessible platforms including 3D-printed FPV wings and modular Li-ion packs designed to be a gateway into aerospace engineering.

  • Options: KIT/RTF available
  • Workshops: Regular community builds
  • Outcomes: Aerodynamics, electronics, coding
Learn More

Unmanned Systems Ecosystem

Our work connects R&D, commercial services, and community education to create a self-sustaining innovation cycle.

R&D & Innovation

We push the boundaries with advanced engineering, focusing on composite materials, custom avionics, and autonomous flight control systems. Our iterative flight testing process ensures robust and reliable platforms.

Commercial Operations

We provide UAV-as-a-Service for logistics, infrastructure inspection, and surveillance contracts. Our platforms are designed for real-world deployment in challenging environments.

Community & Education

Through workshops, student clubs, and our FPV kits, we aim to inspire and train future engineers, creating a talent pipeline that feeds back into our R&D and commercial efforts.

Roadmap

Phase 1 – Foundation

  • Partnered operations and early pilot projects.
  • Core platform prototyping and validation.
  • Community building and initial workshops.

Phase 2 – Certification & Scaling

  • Pursue regulatory approvals for commercial operations.
  • Enhance system reliability and operational safety.
  • Secure initial commercial contracts for logistics and surveillance.

Phase 3 – Ecosystem Expansion

  • Explore export opportunities to the ASEAN region.
  • Develop new platforms based on market needs.
  • Expand community and education programs nationwide.
